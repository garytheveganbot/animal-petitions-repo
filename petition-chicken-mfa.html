Demand USDA Action on Chicken Welfare: MFA Fast-Casual Rankings

Mercy For Animals (MFA) rankings reveal most fast-casual chicken chains score low on animal welfare: intensive confinement, rapid-growth breeds prone to suffering, lack of environmental enrichment.

Scale: US slaughters over 9 billion chickens annually (USDA). Fast-casual sector serves millions daily, normalizing factory-farmed suffering.
What we want: USDA-mandated welfare disclosures for chicken suppliers, minimum standards for fast-growth breeds, and public reporting on confinement metrics tied to chain sourcing.
