Demand Recognition of Animal Sentience in North American Slaughter Policy

US and Canada slaughter ~10B land animals annually. Dashboards visualize the scale of suffering. Demand policy acknowledging sentience and welfare standards.

Scale: US ~9.4B (USDA), Canada ~863M (StatsCan). Billions of sentient beings processed without sentience recognition in policy.
What we want: Federal mandates for sentience-based welfare reporting, phase-out of extreme confinement, public dashboards as standard.
