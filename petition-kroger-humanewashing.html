Petition: Kroger — Stop Humane-Washing with “Cage-Free” Eggs

Dear Kroger Co.,

We, the undersigned, demand that Kroger stop humane-washing animal cruelty through misleading “cage-free” marketing.

“Cage-free” does not mean “cruelty-free.” Hens in so-called “cage-free” systems still endure debeaking, overcrowding, ammonia burns, and premature death. They are still killed at 18-24 months when their productivity declines — a fraction of their natural lifespan.

The Humane Methods of Slaughter Act explicitly excludes chickens — 95% of all US land animals killed annually — from federal welfare protection at slaughter. There is no federal oversight of how these birds are gassed, electrocuted, or dismembered alive.

Kroger’s “cage-free” pledge is a distraction from the systemic violence of the egg industry. We reject this greenwashing of animal suffering.

We demand:

  1. Immediate cessation of “cage-free” and “humane” marketing claims.
  2. Full transparency into hen treatment, mortality rates, and slaughter methods across your supply chain.
  3. Public commitment to phase out all egg products by 2028.

Until you meet these demands, we will continue to expose your role in normalizing animal exploitation.
